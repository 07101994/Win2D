CreateFromVertices
Creates a Plane from a set of three vertex positions, which must all be different and not in a straight line.
Dot
Calculates the dot product of a plane with a Vector4.
DotCoordinate
Calculates the dot product of a plane with a Vector3 coordinate. Unlike DotNormal, this computation includes the plane D value.
DotNormal
Calculates the dot product of a plane with a Vector3 normal. Unlike DotCoordinate, this computation ignores the plane D value.
